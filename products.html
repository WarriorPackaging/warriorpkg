We are a family owned company with over 25 years experience in the packaging industry. Our goal is to provide the best service and quality in our products.

Our Wood Department specializes in custom pallets and crates. One time use or reusable as well as show crates. We are ISP-15 Certified to allow you to ship anywhere around the world.

Our Industrial Shrink Film Department specializes in protecting your equipment whether it be from the sun or rain while shipping, or for your storage, and if necessary we also have desiccant. We are available to do on site shrink wrapping.
